Welcome to Ivy Avenue

Understated elegance, designed in monochrome.

Ivy Avenue is a refined faux-flower brand created for those who appreciate understated elegance. Our collections are intentionally designed in a monochrome palette, allowing form, texture, and craftsmanship to take centre stage.

Every stem is curated to feel timeless—never trend-driven—so your arrangements remain effortlessly sophisticated season after season. With no maintenance required, Ivy Avenue florals offer enduring beauty for modern living.
